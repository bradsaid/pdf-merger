About Our Free PDF Merger

Welcome to our free online PDF merger, designed to make combining PDF files simple and secure. Our tool allows you to merge up to 20 PDFs directly in your browser, with no software installation or file uploads required. Your files stay private, as all processing happens on your device.

Why Choose Us?

  • Privacy First: No files are sent to servers, ensuring your data stays secure.
  • Easy to Use: Drag and drop PDFs to reorder and merge with a single click.
  • Free Forever: Enjoy unlimited merging without sign-ups or hidden fees.
  • Browser-Based: Works on any device, from desktops to mobiles.

Our Mission

We aim to provide a fast, reliable, and user-friendly tool to streamline your PDF workflows, whether for work, school, or personal projects. Built with open-source technology, our merger is designed for everyone.
